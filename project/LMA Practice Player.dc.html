The practice player
Acquire it — don't study it.
Full hi-fi screen set, mobile-first. Five steps in a fixed order — GRASP → HUM → SHADOW → READ → RECALL — and the same ten sentences flow through each in turn. The first three steps are audio-only (ears before eyes); written text first appears in READ, with a transliteration the adventurer can flip on. Coral is the one live signal on any screen — and the only thing that ever moves.
