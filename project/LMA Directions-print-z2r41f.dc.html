LMA · Three visual directions
Same method, three feels
Method is locked across all three: GRASP → HUM → SHADOW → READ → RECALL, 10 sentences flowing through every step in order. GRASP / HUM / SHADOW are audio-only — nothing to read (ears before eyes). Text first appears in READ, with an optional transliteration toggle there. Each step is gated: clear all 10 (4★ or 3 attempts each) to unlock the next. Mic records live while audio plays · forward-only · every take = +1 rep (the hero metric). Only the visual / experiential treatment changes below.
Direction 1
Quiet luxury · editorial
Maximum restraint — one idea per screen, generous negative space, large refined serif. Recording is a soft breathing dot, never a chunky block. Feels like a premium meditation app: calm, confident, expensive.
